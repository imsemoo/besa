International Education Fair

  • Cairo Hilton Heliopolis Hotel

  • 16 February - 17 February

  • Alexandria Hilton Corniche Hotel

  • 19 February

BESA would like to invite you to attend and participate in the International Education Fair – one of the largest educational fairs in MENA region where leading international universities and educational institutions can directly interact with Students.

BESA’s International Educational Fair, is a one stop shop destination for students wishing to have more information on the process of applying for a course abroad and is planned to be as well the main platform for education opportunities abroad.

The fair will provide an opportunity for students, parents, and school administrators to interact with university representatives, learn about the universities' application process, and identify educational programs and resources available to Egyptian/Arab students.

Our fairs will enable students to directly seek information about the study opportunities available at our partner universities and educational institutions, at the same time allow universities and educational institutions to promote for their programs and recruit students. There are more than 130 accredited higher education institutions in the UK, USA, Europe and Asia, and an advisor from BESA can assist students to narrow their options, define their priorities, and plan their college careers.

The International Educational Fair will have demos, discussion, one-to-one sessions, and counseling all under one roof. While the exhibitors get the opportunity to showcase their courses and programs they offer, the student community has a chance to check the offerings and their options and then make an informed choice. Students are given counseling by experienced faculty staff from participating institutions on the courses they should take according to their preferred career choices.
