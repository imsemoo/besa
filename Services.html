Services

The British Educational Services Agency (BESA), was established in 2012 - to be the first Educational agency of its category in Egypt, that is dedicated to providing access to international educational institutions and experiences for students from Egypt and all over the world.

BESA was established initially to assist and provide access to high quality British educational institutions, as the UK is justifiably seen as one of the foremost providers of quality education and training in the world, then expanded to partnership with other international educational providers from over 10 Countries
